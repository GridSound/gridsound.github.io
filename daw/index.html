The GridSound's DAW is moving from  https://gridsound.github.io/daw  --to->  https://gridsound.com/daw


Changing of domain (URL) means losing what you have in your browser's localStorage for gridsound.github.io, there is ONE localStorage per domain.
So if you had some cool local compositions you want to keep, copy the content of the box below...



... and go to https://gridsound.com/daw and paste it in the JavaScript console.


To open the JS console you have to press F12 then go to the Console panel, then ctrl+v and press enter,
after that you can refresh and find your compositions as usual.

If you have any problem during this operation, you can send an email to contact@gridsound.com
